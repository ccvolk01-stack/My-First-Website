Jacob and Esau

Jacob and Esau is a very unique and challenging character to play because you are responsible for controlling two characters simultaneously. This can be difficult since you effectively have two hitboxes, but it also comes with the benefit of dual damage and item synergy. Despite the difficulty, they have some of the most powerful unlocks in the game.
